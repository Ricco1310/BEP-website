The contents of this week

For week 7 and 8 we worked on experience prototyping during which we created a Wizard of Oz prototype. Using this we could test a balancing related game.

Initial Design

For our initial design we decided to use stepping stones to simulate the floor is lava. For this we used processing for its ease of use for animation. For our the floor is lava game we decided to base it on the stepping stones sinking into the lava. To complete the trail the user would have to stay on all of the stepping stones, but no longer then a few seconds.
