Final reflection

The insights about prototyping I have gained have helped me tremendously in other projects and as such I could see the immideate fruit of my labor. I have used lo-fi wireframes to quickly iterate and design my visual designs for ID2, I have used the skills learned during week 7 and 8 for the PDE video and I have learned the general principles behind prototyping. Key insights that I have learned are the simplicity of rapid prototyping, how to make prototypes understandable and how to interview and understand people's opinions of my prototypes. Lastly I have also swapped to a new toolset that enhances my prototypes even further because of this course, seeing as I was hampered by Marvel. I swapped to Invision, for its simplicity, and Axure, for its robustness. This all in all has allowed me to make a better user experience during my prototyping. Which in turn makes my final user experience many times better.
